## Cash vs Investing (Inflation-Adjusted)

**Objective**  
Compare the real (inflation-adjusted) outcome of saving cash versus investing a fixed
monthly amount into an ASX-listed asset using a dollar-cost averaging strategy.

**Key Concepts**
- Dollar-cost averaging
- Inflation adjustment
- Opportunity cost of holding cash

**Tools**
- Python
- pandas
- matplotlib
- yfinance

**Output**
- Line chart comparing real cash value vs real investment value over time
- Final calculation of additional real wealth created by investing
